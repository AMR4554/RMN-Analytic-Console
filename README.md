RMN Advertiser Health & Activation Console

Tech Stack: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Plotly, Dash, Dash Bootstrap Components

Project Overview

Developed an interactive Retail Media Network (RMN) analytics dashboard that helps retail media teams identify advertisers at risk of churn, monitor advertiser health, and discover high-performing tail advertisers ready for budget expansion. The dashboard provides actionable insights to improve advertiser retention, increase ad spend adoption, and support revenue growth initiatives.

Key Features
Built a comprehensive Advertiser Health Score (0–100) using multiple business signals:
Campaign Recency
Spend Trend
ROAS Performance
Campaign Frequency
Onboarding Completion
Classified advertisers into:
Healthy
Watch
At Risk
Identified Ready-to-Scale Advertisers based on:
Above-benchmark ROAS
Growing Spend Trends
Active Campaign Participation
Created interactive KPIs:
Total Spend
Total Sales
Average ROAS
Revenue at Risk
Designed visualizations including:
Advertiser Health Distribution
Spend by Advertiser Tier
ROAS vs Spend Analysis
Top Advertisers Leaderboard
Implemented dynamic filters for:
Advertiser Tier
Health Status
Data Engineering & Analytics
Generated and processed 20,000+ synthetic advertiser records.
Performed data cleaning, validation, missing value handling, and standardization using Pandas.
Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify advertiser behavior patterns and performance trends.
Applied feature engineering techniques to derive health scores, churn indicators, and scale-up opportunities.
Business Impact
Enables proactive identification of advertisers likely to reduce or stop spending.
Helps account managers prioritize retention efforts based on revenue at risk.
Supports growth by surfacing high-potential tail advertisers for upsell opportunities.
Demonstrates how data-driven decision-making can improve advertiser activation and RMN revenue performance.
